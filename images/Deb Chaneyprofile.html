Deb Chaney is a contemporary abstract artist, published author, speaker, & workshop facilitator. Her layered mixed media artwork is inspired by her inner spiritual journey to personal empowerment. Self taught, with a B.Sc. from the University of Victoria in Earth and Ocean Sciences,she began her artistic journey by taking a number of workshops with artists whom she'd admired,and then selling and exhibiting her work at outdoor fairs and cafes.Recently Deb completed the installation of four 16' x 10'hand-painted tiles of her west coast abstract artworks as a large scale outdoor public art installation in North Vancouver in collaboration with Adera Developments (Vancouver) and Viuva Lamego (Portugal).She has been named twice in the media as one of the top 10 contemporary artists to watch in Vancouver.

Deb Chaney has been creating her spiritually inspired mixed media abstract paintings for two decades. From Vancouver, Canada, she exhibits internationally in public art, TV and Film,and her work appears in corporate and private collections.Her paintings are composed of many intriguing layers and textures.Her creative process supports her being in the present moment and is part of her healing journey.
